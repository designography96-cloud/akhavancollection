Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Controller

The controller responsible for data processing on this website is:

Akhavan Collection
Mittersteig 15, 1040 Wien, Austria
Email: info@akhavancollection.com

2. Data We Collect

When you use this website, the following data may be collected:

  • Information you provide when submitting the venue rental enquiry form (name, email address, event details)
  • Booking information submitted through the Calendly reservation system
  • Standard server log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, time of visit)

3. Purpose of Processing

We process your personal data solely for the purposes for which it was provided: to respond to venue rental enquiries, to manage visit reservations, and to maintain the security and operation of this website.

4. Legal Basis

Processing is carried out on the basis of Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of a contract or pre-contractual measures) and Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interests) for server log data.

5. Third-Party Services

This website uses Calendly (Calendly LLC, 271 17th St NW, Atlanta, GA 30363, USA) to manage visit reservations. When you use the booking widget, your data is processed by Calendly according to their own privacy policy, available at calendly.com/privacy.

This website embeds Google Maps (Google LLC, 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA) to display our location. Your IP address and browser data may be transmitted to Google when the map loads.

6. Data Retention

Enquiry data is retained for as long as necessary to respond to and fulfil your request, and deleted thereafter unless a longer retention period is required by law.

7. Your Rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing or request restriction
  • Lodge a complaint with the Austrian Data Protection Authority (Datenschutzbehörde)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address above.

8. Cookies

This website does not use tracking or analytics cookies. Session data may be stored temporarily by embedded third-party services (Calendly, Google Maps) as described above.
